# Scheme Name: Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G)
State: Central Government
Sector: Rural Housing

## Description
Aims to provide a pucca house with basic amenities to all houseless households and those living in kutcha and dilapidated houses.

## Eligibility Criteria
- Families identified based on SECC 2011 data (Socio-Economic Caste Census).
- Houseless families or families living in zero, one, or two-room kutcha houses.
- No motorized two-wheeler/three-wheeler/fishing boat.
- No member earning more than ₹10,000 per month.
- No Kisan Credit Card with limit above ₹50,000.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance of **₹1,20,000** in plain areas and **₹1,30,000** in hilly states for construction of house.
- Additional assistance of ₹12,000 for toilet construction under Swachh Bharat Mission.
- 90-95 days of unskilled labour wage under MGNREGA.
