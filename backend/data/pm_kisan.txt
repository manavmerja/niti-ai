# Scheme Name: Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
State: Central Government (India)
Sector: Agriculture / Farmers

## Description
PM-KISAN is a central sector scheme to provide income support to all landholding farmers' families in the country to supplement their financial needs for procuring various inputs related to agriculture.

## Eligibility Criteria
1. **Eligible:**
   - All landholding farmer families having cultivable land holding in their names.
2. **Not Eligible (Exclusions):**
   - Institutional Land holders.
   - Farmer families holding constitutional posts.
   - Serving or retired officers/employees of State/Central Government.
   - Doctors, Engineers, Lawyers, CAs, Architects.
   - Income Tax payers.

## Benefits
- Financial benefit of ₹6,000 per year.
- Payable in three equal installments of ₹2,000 each, every four months.
- Amount is transferred directly into the bank accounts of the beneficiaries (DBT).

## Documents Required
- Aadhaar Card
- Land Ownership Papers (Khasra/Khatauni)
- Bank Account Details
- Mobile Number linked with Aadhaar

## Application Process
1. Farmers can register on the official portal under 'New Farmer Registration'.
2. Can also register via PM-KISAN Mobile App.
3. Visit the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC).
