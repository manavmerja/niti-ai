# Scheme Name: PM Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)
State: Central Government
Sector: Women / Energy / BPL

## Description
Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana aims to provide clean cooking fuel (LPG) to rural and deprived households who are otherwise using traditional cooking fuels (wood, coal, dung cakes) which are harmful to health.

## Eligibility Criteria
- Adult woman belonging to a poor household (BPL).
- Must not have an existing LPG connection in the household.
- Priority to SC/ST, Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY), Forest Dwellers, etc.

- KYC of the applicant.
- Ration Card / Family composition document.
