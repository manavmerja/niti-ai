# Scheme Name: PM Vishwakarma Yojana
State: Central Government
Sector: Skill Development / Artisans

## Description
A new central sector scheme to support traditional artisans and craftspeople (Vishwakarmas) working with their hands and tools.

## Eligibility Criteria
- Artisans working in 18 traditional trades (Carpenter, Boat Maker, Blacksmith, Potter, Sculptor, Cobbler, Mason, Barber, Washerman, Tailor, etc.).
- Minimum age: 18 years.
- Should not have availed loans under PMEGP, PM SVANidhi, or Mudra in the past 5 years.

## Benefits
- **Recognition:** PM Vishwakarma Certificate and ID Card.
- **Skill Upgradation:** Basic Training (5-7 days) with a stipend of ₹500 per day.
- **Toolkit Incentive:** Grant of ₹15,000 for buying modern tools.
- **Credit Support:** Collateral-free loans up to ₹1 lakh (First Tranche) and ₹2 lakh (Second Tranche) at a concessional interest rate of 5%.
