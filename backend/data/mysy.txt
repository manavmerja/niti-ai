# Scheme Name: Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana (MYSY)
State: Gujarat
Sector: Education / Scholarship

## Description
MYSY is a scholarship scheme by the Government of Gujarat to help economically weaker but meritorious students pursue higher education (Diploma, Engineering, Medical, etc.).

## Eligibility Criteria
1. **Academic:**
   - For Bachelor's Course: 80 percentile or more in Std 12 (Science/General Stream).
   - For Diploma Course: 80 percentile or more in Std 10.
   - For D-to-D (Diploma to Degree): 65% or more in Diploma.
2. **Income:**
   - Annual family income must be less than ₹6,00,000 (6 Lakhs).
3. **Other:**
   - Student must be a resident of Gujarat.
   - Not applicable for Post-Graduation courses (only for UG).

## Benefits
1. **Tuition Fee Support:**
   - Medical/Dental: 50% of fees or ₹2,00,000 (whichever is less).
   - Engineering/Pharmacy/Professional: 50% of fees or ₹50,000 (whichever is less).
   - Diploma: 50% of fees or ₹25,000 (whichever is less).
   - BA/BCom/BSc: 50% of fees or ₹10,000 (whichever is less).
2. **Hostel Support:**
   - ₹1,200 per month (₹12,000 per year) for students studying outside their taluka and staying in private hostels/PG.
3. **Book/Instrument Assistance:**
   - One-time grant: ₹10,000 (Medical), ₹5,000 (Engineering), ₹3,000 (Diploma).

## Documents Required
- Aadhaar Card (Student & Parents)
- Income Certificate (Mamlatdar/TDO)
- 10th / 12th Marksheet
- Admission Letter from College
- Fee Receipts (Tuition Fee)
- Bank Passbook (First Page)
- Self Declaration Form
