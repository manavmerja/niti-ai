# Scheme Name: Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)
State: Central Government
Sector: Women & Child Development / Savings

## Description
A small deposit scheme for the girl child launched as a part of the 'Beti Bachao Beti Padhao' campaign. It offers a high interest rate and tax benefits.

## Eligibility Criteria
- Parents or legal guardians can open an account in the name of a girl child.
- The girl child must be below the age of **10 years**.
- Only one account per girl child. Maximum two accounts per family (for two daughters).

## Benefits
- High Interest Rate (currently around 8.2% per annum).
- Tax Deduction benefit under Section 80C (up to ₹1.5 lakh).
- Maturity period is 21 years from the date of opening the account.
- Partial withdrawal (50%) allowed for the girl's higher education after she turns 18.

## Documents Required
- Birth Certificate of the girl child.
- ID and Address proof of the parent/guardian (Aadhaar/PAN).
- Deposit amount (Min ₹250).
