# Scheme Name: Digital India Internship Scheme
State: Central Government (MeitY)
Sector: Education / Internship

## Description
An internship program by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) for Indian students to gain practical work experience in Digital India projects.

## Eligibility Criteria
- Indian students from recognized universities in India.
- Must be pursuing B.E./B.Tech (3rd/4th year) or M.E./M.Tech/M.Sc (Electronics/CS/IT).
- Minimum **60% marks** in the last degree or semester.
